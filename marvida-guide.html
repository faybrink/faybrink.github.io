About the Community

Marvida: Live. Play. Relax.

Marvida is one of the Cypress corridor's newest master-planned communities, developed by Starwood Land and anchored off Westgreen Boulevard near Grand Parkway 99. New homes are open daily across more than ten builder model rows, ranging from the high $200s well into the $500s depending on the collection and lot.

I live here. I also hold a listing agent relationship with Clay Residential for the Marvida corridor, which means I work alongside the builder community on a regular basis — not just as a buyer's agent passing through.

The Island Amenity Village

Now Open

The Island Amenity Village is the centerpiece of Marvida — a resort-style complex that residents can use from day one. It's not a future promise. It opened in 2025.

  • Lazy river
  • Splash pad
  • Lap pool
  • Tennis courts
  • Pickleball courts
  • Sand volleyball
  • Dog park (leash-free)
  • Event lawn
  • Shaded pavilion
  • Workout facilities
  • Green Bolt Trail
  • Clubhouse

Marvida residents also have access to amenities at sister communities Miramesa, Canyon Lakes West, and Bridge Creek — that's 4 parks, 3 clubhouses, 3 pools, and 3 workout facilities total.

Location

Everything Within Reach
